# TMAY (Tell Me About Yourself) — 스피킹 스크립트

> 구조: **Present → Past → Future**. 슬라이드 활동은 1~2번(Present, Past)까지.
> 원칙: 짧은 문장 / 쉬운 단어 / 숫자 1~2개만. 소리 내어 읽는 용도.

---

## 1. Present — Who I am now (the headline)

> *"I'm a [role] with [X years] of experience in [domain], specializing in [key strengths]."*

**Script (약 20초)**

> "I'm an AI product engineer and an educator.
> I build AI tools — and I teach people how to use them.
> Right now I lead a small AI team at a business research institute in Korea.
> My strength is simple: I take something complex, and I make it easy to understand."

**변형 (더 짧게, 10초)**

> "I'm an AI engineer who builds products and teaches. I turn complex AI into something anyone can understand."

---

## 2. Past — The 2–3 experiences that shaped me

> *"I started my career in [area] at [company], where I developed [skill]. From there, I moved into [next role], where I focused on [responsibility] and achieved [impact]."*

**Script (약 60초) — 3개의 점, 하나의 선**

> **(1) Teaching first.**
> "I started as a teacher, not an engineer.
> For four years I ran a small online English school from Vietnam.
> I connected Korean students with Filipino teachers.
> I learned one thing there: if the learner doesn't get it, it's my problem — not theirs."
>
> **(2) Then, engineering for non-engineers.**
> "Later I joined a Microsoft support team.
> My customers were not developers. They were normal office workers.
> I had to explain hard technical things in plain words.
> I became number one on my team in every metric."
>
> **(3) Now, building and teaching together.**
> "Today I build AI products, and I turn each one into a lesson.
> For example, I built a machine-learning app that predicts apartment prices in Seoul.
> But I didn't stop there. I broke it into ten levels — from a simple line, up to AutoML — so a beginner can walk through it step by step.
> That is the thread in my whole career: **build it, then teach it.**"
